CAPTCHA SECURITY GENERATION AND VALIDATION

Web security has become a critical and challenging issue due to the growth of Internet. BOT is one of the most malicious threats to the web services. It runs automated tasks over the internet and creates problem. One solution for securing web from BOT is to develop CAPTCHA.
